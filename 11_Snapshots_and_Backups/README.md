# Snapshots and Backups

## Why this topic exists
Servers can fail or be deleted accidentally.
Backups protect data.

## What is a snapshot?
A snapshot is a backup of an EBS volume at a point in time.

## Key properties
- Incremental after first snapshot
- Stored by AWS
- Used for recovery and cloning

## Why snapshots matter
- Disaster recovery
- Restore deleted data
- Create AMIs

## Real-world analogy
A snapshot is like taking a photo of your hard disk.

## Diagram
EBS Volume → Snapshot → Restore

## Interview explanation
"Snapshots are incremental backups of EBS volumes used for data protection and recovery."
